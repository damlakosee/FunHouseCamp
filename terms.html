Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: January 2025

Important: By booking a place at Funhouse Camp, you agree to these terms and conditions. Please read them carefully.

1. About Our Services

Funhouse Camp provides non-residential school holiday activity camps. Sessions involve activities and supervision by staff who are all DBS-certified and are the main responsible adults during the session.

2. Booking, Payment, and Cancellation

  • Admission: A child will only be allowed to attend once full payment has been received. Places are sold on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Payment Deadline: Payment must be received 14 days prior to the club start date, unless alternative arrangements have been agreed upon booking.
  • Cancellation by Parent: If Funhouse Camp receives a cancellation request at least 48 hours in advance of the club start time, we will offer a full refund.
  • Cancellation by Funhouse Camp: If Funhouse Camp cancels a club for any reason, we will issue a full refund within 28 days.

3. Medical Information and Health

First Aid

A Paediatric First Aider will be present at every session. By agreeing to these terms, you give permission for Funhouse Camp staff to administer first aid to your child should injury or illness occur.

Allergies & No-Nut Policy

⚠️ IMPORTANT: All Funhouse Camp clubs are strictly No-Nut zones. You must not pack any nut-containing foods for your child.

Illness

Your child must not attend if they have a fever, severe cough, or have experienced vomiting or diarrhoea in the past 24 hours.

4. Supervision and Behaviour

Child Protection

All Funhouse Camp coaches have attended Safeguarding training. Any child protection issues will be referred to the Safeguarding Officer.

Unacceptable Behaviour

Funhouse Camp reserves the right to ask a parent to remove a child without notice if the child's behaviour is unmanageable.

5. Drop-off and Collection

Late Collection Policy

We require all children to be collected promptly at the scheduled time. A late pick-up fee will be charged immediately for any late collection.
