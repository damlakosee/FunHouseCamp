Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Your Privacy Matters: Funhouse Camp is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you register your child for Funhouse Camp, we collect the following information:

Child Information:

  • Full name and date of birth
  • Medical information (allergies, conditions, medications)
  • Dietary requirements
  • Emergency contact details

Parent/Guardian Information:

  • Full name and contact details
  • Email address and phone number
  • Home address

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Camp Operations: To provide a safe experience
  • Health & Safety: To respond to medical needs
  • Compliance: To meet legal and Ofsted requirements

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
