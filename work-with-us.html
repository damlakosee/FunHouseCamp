Work With Us!

At Funhouse Camp, we look for people who are upbeat, responsible, and dedicated to helping children have amazing holidays. If you enjoy working with young people, can think on your feet, and are eager to develop your skills, you'll thrive here! We are a passionate team dedicated to delivering unforgettable, exciting experiences.

Current Roles

Qualified Teacher / HLTA

Age Requirement: 18+

Key Requirement

Relevant qualification required

Group Leader

Age Requirement: 18+

Key Requirement

Confident managing groups up to 20 children

Role Details

  • Reports to: Venue Leader
  • You will: Supervise and care for a group of children, confidently lead and adapt activities, help new or anxious children settle in, and ensure full safeguarding/welfare for every child.
  • Essentials: Must be 18+ and suitable to work with children (subject to Enhanced DBS).

Junior Admin Assistant (Under 18)

Age Requirement: 13+

Role Details

  • Reports to: Venue Leader
  • You will: Support the smooth running of the camp by assisting with basic admin tasks, helping set up and pack away equipment, checking stock, and assisting Group Leaders during activities.
  • Essentials: Must be 13+ with Parental Consent. (Safe recruitment checks apply for 16+).

Our Safeguarding Commitment (The Non-Negotiable)

Funhouse Enterprises Ltd is fully committed to safeguarding all children in our care. We expect every member of our staff to share this commitment and follow our policies and training. Our recruitment practices are designed to ensure that only suitable individuals work with the children who attend our camps.
